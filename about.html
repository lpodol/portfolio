ABOUT

A writer and web developer who believes in communicating with the same clarity in both written English and code, Lauren is a student in the Web Development Immersive (WDI) program at General Assembly.

Lauren previously wrote children's book reviews for the Junior Library Guild and various online publications. She fell in love with code while helping a friend launch Bee Software, for which she learned basic Perl scripting. During her time at General Assembly, she plans to gain a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Ruby.

Lauren holds a Bachelor of Arts in history from Brown University and a Master's in Social Work from New York University. She lives in Brooklyn with her significant other, Andrew, and a large, hungry dog named Pelle.
